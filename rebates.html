Heat Pump Rebates in British Columbia

CleanBC and federal incentive programs can provide up to $24,500 toward a new heat pump installation. We complete an in-home assessment, confirm eligibility, and submit all documentation so you receive the maximum funding available.

Available programs

Program Maximum rebate Eligibility
CleanBC Better Homes $6,000 Primary residence, replacing fossil fuel heating with a heat pump system.
Federal Greener Homes $5,000 Pre- and post-retrofit EnerGuide evaluations and eligible cold-climate heat pump equipment.
Income-qualified top-ups Up to $18,500 Households that meet CleanBC income thresholds for enhanced rebates.

How APEX helps

We provide free consultations, energy advisor referrals, and detailed quotes that outline eligible rebate amounts. Our office handles the application paperwork and tracks milestones to ensure funds are released quickly after installation.
