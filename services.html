Heat Pump Services

We design, install, and maintain efficient heat pump systems for homeowners and strata buildings across Metro Vancouver. Our technicians are fully licensed, insured, and CleanBC and HPCN certified.

What we offer

  • Home comfort upgrades: Ductless and ducted heat pump installations tailored to your floor plan and insulation needs.
  • Gas furnace retrofits: Remove old fossil fuel systems and transition to all-electric heating while keeping existing ductwork.
  • Emergency repairs: 24/7 service trucks with common parts stocked to minimize downtime.
  • Seasonal maintenance: Tune-ups, coil cleaning, and refrigerant checks to maintain peak performance.

We partner with leading manufacturers including Mitsubishi, Daikin, and Airlux to deliver quiet, reliable comfort. Our in-house team handles permitting, rebate paperwork, and electrical coordination.
